How to Play

Reconnaissance chess is a variation of chess that operates under the fundamental principle of Kriegspiel (another variant): your opponents moves are hidden. Instead, each turn you are given the chance to reveal a 3x3 grid of the "true" board before making a normal move.

Getting Started

  1. Start by clicking the button to create a new game
  2. Copy and share the new url with your opponent
  3. The game will shortly start. Have Fun!
